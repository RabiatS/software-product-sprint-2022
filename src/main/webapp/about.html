About Us

Our mission is to let people talk to each other, freely.

Being able to communicate easily with others shouldn't have to be a financial issue. So, as a group of students in the Google SPS Program, we created Talky Talky, in order to improve accessibility to communication for people that need it. Anyone can create a card with an image attached to it, and tap or click on it order to hear the word played back to them via text-to-speech. Create an account and try it out!
